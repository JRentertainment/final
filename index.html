Today, hip hop continues to evolve, with artists experimenting with new sounds, styles, and production techniques. Some of the most popular sub-genres of hip hop today include trap, drill, and mumble rap. These styles are characterized by their heavy use of 808 drums, auto-tuned vocals, and trap melodies. Overall, the sound of hip hop has evolved significantly over the years, reflecting changes in technology, culture, and artistic expression. Despite these changes, hip hop remains one of the most influential and popular genres of music in the world today.
